Ineffable - Incapable of being expressed in words; indescribable.
Ephemeral - Lasting for a very short time; fleeting.
Perspicacious - Having a keen understanding; perceptive.
Obdurate - Unyielding; stubborn.
Profligate - Extravagant; wasteful.

Esoteric - Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest.
Salubrious - Conducive to or promoting health or well-being.
Plethora - An excessive amount or an overabundance.
Lucid - Clear and easy to understand; plain.
Equanimity - The quality of being calm and even-tempered; composure.
